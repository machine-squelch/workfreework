## HTTPS Setup (Critical!)

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Open GitHub**
   - Go to https://github.com/machine-squelch/workfreework
   - Click "Settings" (top menu)

2. **Navigate to Pages**
   - In left sidebar, click "Pages"
   - Under "Build and deployment"

3. **Verify Custom Domain**
   - Should show: `workfreework.com`
   - If not, add it and save

4. **Enable HTTPS**
   - Check the box: ✅ "Enforce HTTPS"
   - You might see "Not yet available" (normal)
   - Wait 10-60 minutes for certificate provisioning

5. **Verify It Works**
   - Visit https://workfreework.com
   - Should see 🔒 in address bar
   - http:// should redirect to https://

**Troubleshooting:**
- If certificate won't provision after 24 hours:
  - Remove custom domain
  - Wait 5 minutes
  - Re-add custom domain
  - Wait for provisioning

Full guide: See `HTTPS_SETUP.md`
