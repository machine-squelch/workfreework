# Company Facts

- Creator: Adam — a builder who needed to be free and chart his own path.
- Ethos: Work should expand your freedom, not shrink it. We favor simple, practical systems that help people own their time and outcomes.
- Perspective: The current form of capitalism has concentrated outsized gains among a small elite. We believe in rebalancing power toward creators and independents, and pushing back on systems that keep people down.
- What we’re building: Tools, playbooks, and guidance to help people create independent income streams, price their value confidently, and navigate the business side without getting trapped in bureaucracy.
- Voice: Clear, direct, human. We avoid jargon. We bias for action and usefulness over status-signaling.
- Promise: Respect attention. Be honest about tradeoffs. Help people move one step closer to practical freedom.
